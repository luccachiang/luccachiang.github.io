Hi there! I am a first-year MSCS student at UC San Diego, working with Prof. Xiaolong Wang.

Previously, I was a research intern at Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute, fortunate to work with Prof. Huazhe Xu at Tsinghua University from June 2023 to October 2024. I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Sichuan University, collaborating with Prof. Yan Wang.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, and Robotics. I am passionate about integrating Visual intelligence into Robots. Feel free to reach out via email if you are interested into my research :)
