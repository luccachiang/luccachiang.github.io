Hi there! I am an MSCS graduate at UC San Diego, starting in 2024 Fall. I am also a research intern at Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute, advised by Prof. Huazhe Xu at Tsinghua University.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Sichuan University, working with Prof. Yan Wang.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, and Robotics. I am passionate about integrating Visual intelligence into Robots. Feel free to reach out via email if you'd like to connect :)
